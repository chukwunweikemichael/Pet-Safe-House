You'll want to know these pet ID tips

If your four-legged friend goes missing, do you have a plan?

Animal Humane Society stopped by a popular dog-walking spot in Minneapolis to see what pet owners know about microchips, ID tags, and locating a lost animal.

The Fourth of July is one of the most common times of the year for pets to go missing. As Independence Day approaches, be sure you're prepared should the unthinkable happen. Find out more resources for your lost pet.
